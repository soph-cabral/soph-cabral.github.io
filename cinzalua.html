CinzaLua Studio

MIAMI BORN START-UP

CinzaLua Studio originated in Miami, founded by architectural/computational designer Sophia Cabral in 2022. The studio has formed collaborations with many designers, artists, international manufacturers, and craftsmen. It is a multi-media studio with a focus on technology, digital fabrication, and story-telling.
