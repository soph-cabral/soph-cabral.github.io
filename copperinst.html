Clafoutis Sonata

In fall 2023, Vizcaya’s historic kitchen became the site of a commission by Karla Caprali and Sophia Cabral, a mother-daughter collective working together as Caprali and Cinza Lua Studios. They are creating a video installation with multiple independent screens that will play handcrafted animations. Responding to the theme of the kitchen is the heart of the home, Caprali and Cabral’s installation conjures up a reimagined active Gilded Age kitchen by weaving in playful animations with Vizcaya’s past. The installation incorporates mixed media, including copper designed armature, handcrafted illustrations, and music by Caprali and Miami local string quartet Baby B Strings.
