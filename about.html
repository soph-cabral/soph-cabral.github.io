SOPHIA CABRAL

I grew up in the northern part of Brazil on the edge of the Amazon, surrounded by nature in the city of Belém. I am passionate about making cities a place where every species can feel part of the landscape and enjoy it equally. Currently, I am captivated by the concept of "urban habitats" — pockets of space that foster diverse ecological interactions, ultimately enhancing the lives of all species within a city (not just humans). My aspiration is to construct numerous urban habitats, effectively blurring the boundaries between nature and the cityscape
